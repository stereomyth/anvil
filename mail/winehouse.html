An evening with "The Moneypennies" and Special Guest - 21st June 2012

The Moneypennies have chosen a very special Lady to join them this month!

Cath Nightingale, sister of Maxine Nightingale, famous for her 1976 million-selling top 10 hit 'Get right back to where we started from', joins the guys this month.

Cath has one of those voices that makes the hairs on the back of your neck stand up! Her immensely powerful soul voice, along the lines of Ruby Turner and Beverley Knight will lend itself perfectly to the Moneypennies. It is Oz who is particularly excited this month, as his blues orientated piano style will be complemented by Cath's soulful voice. She has performed as a professional singer for over 20 years, in high end Soul Bands on the function circuit, performing at the Jam House and The Cork Guinness Jazz Festival on a number of occasions.
